Platform

Everything your delivery pipeline was missing.

From commit to production — PipeWarden observes, predicts, and optimizes every step so your team ships faster with fewer surprises.

AI pipeline optimization

Detect slow stages, cache misses, and dependency bottlenecks. PipeWarden rewrites pipeline configs to cut build time without breaking anything.

Release risk scoring

Each deploy gets a real-time risk score based on code change, test coverage, deploy history, and downstream dependencies.

Failure prediction

Surface flaky tests, unstable infra, and regression patterns before they become outages. Stop debugging production.

Rollback intelligence

When things go wrong, PipeWarden recommends the exact commit to roll back to — with blast radius and recovery ETA.

Multi-environment visibility

One pane of glass across dev, staging, canary, and prod. See drift, divergence, and mismatched configs in real time.

Incident insights

Auto-generate incident timelines with the deploys, configs, and services involved. Blameless post-mortems in minutes.

Deployment analytics that actually mean something

DORA metrics out of the box, plus PipeWarden's own AI benchmarks comparing your team's velocity, stability, and risk posture to peers of similar scale — updated continuously, not quarterly.

How it works

From connect to confident deploy in under 10 minutes.

No agent sprawl. No invasive refactors. Plug in, and PipeWarden starts learning the shape of your delivery.

Connect repos & runners

One-click OAuth with GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket, Jenkins, or self-hosted runners. Read-only by default.

Analyze pipelines

FAQ

Questions, answered.

How long does setup take?

Most teams are running under 10 minutes. Connect your repo provider via OAuth, optionally install the GitHub App or GitLab token, and PipeWarden starts ingesting your pipeline history automatically.

Which CI/CD platforms do you support?

GitHub Actions, GitLab CI/CD, Bitbucket Pipelines, Jenkins, Azure DevOps, and CircleCI. We also integrate with ArgoCD, Kubernetes, AWS, Azure, and GCP for runtime visibility.

How is my data secured?

On Free + Pro your source code never leaves your machine — the CLI runs locally and only finding metadata is uploaded. AES-256-GCM credential vault. SOC 2 Type I audit is on the roadmap (Q3 2026); we are not certified today. See the full posture on the security page. Customer-managed keys + self-host are available on Team / Enterprise.

Will this change my existing pipelines?

No. PipeWarden is read-only by default. Automation and policy gates are opt-in, per-repo, and fully reversible — you stay in control.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes. Starter is free forever. Growth includes a 14-day trial with no credit card required. Enterprise teams can request a full POC with a dedicated engineer.

What team sizes is PipeWarden built for?

Indie developers and teams of 5–50, today. We're in active build mode with an early-access cohort — join the design-partner program for Pro free for life in exchange for product feedback. Larger orgs are welcome on the self-hosted track.
