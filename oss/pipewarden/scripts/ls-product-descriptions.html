Product Description
PipeWarden is the DevSecOps pipeline orchestrator that watches what your CI/CD pipelines actually do — not just what's in the code. Connect GitHub Actions, GitLab CI/CD, Bitbucket Pipelines, Jenkins, Azure DevOps, and CircleCI in one place. Claude AI flags risky behavior, the DLP scanner catches leaked secrets across 13 patterns, OPA policies block unsafe builds before they ship, and SARIF export wires findings straight into your GitHub Security tab. Think CrowdStrike for CI/CD — multi-platform, AI-powered, audit-ready for SOC2, HIPAA, GDPR, and PCI-DSS.
