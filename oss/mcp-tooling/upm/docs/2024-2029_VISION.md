# UPM 2024-2029 Strategic Vision

## The Future of Software Supply Chain Security

---

## Executive Summary

By 2029, software will consume 99% of all digital interaction. Open-source and third-party dependencies will power 99.9% of that software. UPM will be the platform that secures this foundation—the operating system for software supply chains.

**Our Vision**: To become the default, trusted authority for software dependency security worldwide.

**Our Mission**: To secure the software that runs the world by making dependency security invisible, intelligent, and automated.

## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Platform giants enter our market

**Mitigation**:
- Deep technical moat (AI models)
- Community first (giants are bad at this)
- Enterprise relationships (stickiness)
- Acquisition insurance (become too expensive to copy)

### Risk 2: Open-source alternatives mature

**Mitigation**:
- Innovate faster than they can copy
- Offer features they can't (AI at scale)
- Build community they can't (trust, support)
- monetize things they can't (enterprise features)

### Risk 3: Regulatory changes

**Mitigation**:
- Shape regulations (be at the table)
- Multi-jurisdiction compliance
- Policy flexibility (engine can adapt)
- Advisory board (former regulators)

### Risk 4: Talent acquisition

**Mitigation**:
- Mission-driven company (purpose > money)
- Remote-first (global talent pool)
- Developer-first culture (ship fast)
- Equity for all (ownership mentality)
