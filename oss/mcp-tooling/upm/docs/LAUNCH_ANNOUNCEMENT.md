## The Problem

Modern software depends on open-source. **97% of applications contain open-source code.** The average enterprise application has **5,000+ dependencies**. Yet most organizations have visibility into less than **10%** of what's actually running.

This is the software supply chain crisis.

Recent attacks like SolarWinds, Log4Shell, and Spring4Shell have demonstrated the devastating impact of supply chain vulnerabilities. Organizations struggle with:

- **Fragmented tools** (5+ scanners for different ecosystems)
- **False positives** (40%+ waste engineering time)
- **Manual remediation** (6+ weeks to fix vulnerabilities)
- **No visibility** (into transitive dependencies)

---

**UPM is the first platform that:**
