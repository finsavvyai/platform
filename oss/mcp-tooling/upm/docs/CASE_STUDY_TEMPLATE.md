## Example: Fintech Giant Case Study

### Customer: Global Payments Inc.

**Profile:**
- Fortune 500 financial services
- 2,000+ developers
- 5,000+ repositories
- Java, Python, Node.js stack

**Challenge:**
- Post-SolarWinds mandate to secure supply chain
- Using 5 different SCA tools
- 12-week average remediation time
- SOC 2 audit preparation took 2 weeks

**Solution:**
- Enterprise UPM deployment (self-hosted)
- All repositories connected
- Custom policies for financial regulations
- SSO integration with Okta

**Results:**
- Consolidated to 1 platform (saved $500K/year)
- Reduced remediation to 3 days (95% improvement)
- SOC 2 prep: 2 hours (99% reduction)
- Zero security incidents in 18 months
- $3.2M total annual savings

**Quote:**
> "UPM paid for itself in the first month. The automated remediation alone saves us 500+ engineering hours per month. Our security team can finally focus on strategy instead of manual triage."
