# 🛡️ THE UPM MANIFESTO

---

## We Believe

**Software feeds the world.**

From the coffee we order to the planes we fly, from the hospitals that heal us to the banks that protect our savings—software makes modern life possible.

And software? It's built on dependencies.

**97% of applications contain open-source code.**

Thousands of packages. Millions of lines of code. Written by strangers. Maintained by volunteers. Downloaded a billion times a day.

**This is the foundation of modern civilization.**

And here's the scary part:

**We don't know what's in it.**

---

## The Crisis

The software supply chain is the new frontier of cyberwar.

Attackers don't need to breach your perimeter anymore. They just need to compromise one dependency. One package. One library.

And the whole world comes crashing down.

SolarWinds. Log4Shell. Spring4Shell. Codecov.

Each attack more devastating than the last.

Each proof that **we're building on sand.**

---

## The Problem

Why is this happening?

Because we're optimizing for speed, not security.

Because we're copying from Stack Overflow, not checking provenance.

Because we're `npm install`-ing blindly, hoping for the best.

Because we have 10 different security tools and none of them talk to each other.

Because our developers are overworked and our security teams are overwhelmed.

**Because we've accepted vulnerability debt as the cost of doing business.**

**It's not. It's a ticking time bomb.**

---

## Our Commitment

We're building UPM to fix this.

But UPM is more than a product. It's a statement of beliefs.

## We Believe

### 1. Visibility Is Security

You can't secure what you can't see.

**Every dependency should be known.**
**Every vulnerability should be disclosed.**
**Every license should be documented.**

No more shadow IT. No more `node_modules` of unknown origin. No more "we'll check later."

Later is too late.

---

### 2. Intelligence Over Information

Alert fatigue is real.

10,000 vulnerabilities. 40% false positives. Developers who stopped caring years ago.

**Security teams drowning in noise.**

We believe in intelligence over information.

**Tell me what matters.**
**Tell me what to fix first.**
**Tell me what can wait.**

Context. Prioritization. Actionability.

This is how security actually works.

---

### 3. Automation Is the Only Scale

Manual remediation doesn't scale.

You can't manually fix 5,000 dependencies.
You can't manually review every PR.
You can't manually track every CVE.

**We need automation.**

Smart automation. Safe automation. Automation that respects your workflow.

**Machines should do the boring work.**
**Humans should do the thinking.**

---

### 4. Developers Are Not the Enemy

Security teams: Stop blaming developers.

Developers aren't careless. They're overworked.
They aren't ignorant. They're overwhelmed.
They aren't reckless. They're rushing to ship.

**We've made security hard. We've made it slow. We've made it a blocker.**

**No wonder developers hate us.**

We believe in **security that developers love.**

Security that catches issues before commit.
Security that fixes problems with one click.
Security that's invisible, integrated, helpful.

**When security is easy, everyone is more secure.**

---

### 5. Open Source Deserves Our Support

Millions of developers. Billions of downloads. Trillions in value created.

And what do maintainers get?

**Burnout. Complaints. Zero pay.**

This is unsustainable.

**We believe in funding the future.**

Open source maintainers should be paid.
Open source projects should be sustainable.
Open source security should be free.

**UPM is free for open source. Forever.**

---

### 6. Compliance Shouldn't Be a Nightmare

SOC 2. PCI DSS. GDPR. HIPAA. FedRAMP.

Requirements that grow every year.
Audits that take months.
Spreadsheets that never end.

**We believe in compliance by default.**

One click SBOM generation.
Audit-ready reports on demand.
Immutable logs for regulators.

**Security that's compliant without trying.**

---

### 7. No More False Choices

Speed vs. Security.
Innovation vs. Compliance.
Openness vs. Protection.

**These aren't tradeoffs. They're false choices.**

You can have it all.

**Ship fast. Ship secure.**
** innovate freely. comply easily.**
**Be open. Be protected.**
