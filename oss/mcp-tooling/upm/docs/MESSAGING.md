## Voice & Tone

### Personality Attributes
- **Confident but not arrogant** (We know our stuff, we're not jerks about it)
- **Technical but not jargon-heavy** (We're engineers, but we speak human)
- **Urgent but not alarmist** (This matters, but you're not going to die today)
- **Professional but approachable** (We take security seriously, we don't take ourselves seriously)

### Do's
- Use "we" and "you" (build connection)
- Be specific and concrete (prove it works)
- Show empathy (we've been there)
- Celebrate developers (you're the heroes)

### Don'ts
- Fear-monger (scare tactics backfire)
- Over-promise (under-deliver kills trust)
- Bash competitors (elevate, don't hate)
- Be boring (engineering deserves personality)
