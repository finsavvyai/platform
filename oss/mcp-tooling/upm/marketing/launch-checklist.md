# 🚀 UPM Product Launch Checklist

## Pre-Launch (2 Weeks Before)

### Content Preparation
- [ ] Launch announcement blog post written
- [ ] Product Hunt description (500 words)
- [ ] Hacker News "Show HN" post drafted
- [ ] Launch video script written
- [ ] Launch video recorded and edited
- [ ] Social media posts scheduled
- [ ] Email templates prepared

### Technical Preparation
- [ ] Website ready and tested
- [ ] Signup flow tested
- [ ] Documentation complete
- [ ] Demo environment ready
- [ ] Support system ready
- [ ] Analytics tracking verified

### Community Preparation
- [ ] Beta users notified
- [ ] Early access list prepared
- [ ] Community guidelines published
- [ ] Support team briefed
- [ ] FAQ document ready

---

## Launch Day (Day 1)

### Morning (9 AM EST)
- [ ] Product Hunt launch (9 AM sharp)
- [ ] Hacker News "Show HN" post (10 AM)
- [ ] Twitter announcement
- [ ] LinkedIn post
- [ ] Email to beta users
- [ ] Reddit posts (r/programming, r/java, r/Python)

### Throughout Day
- [ ] Monitor all channels
- [ ] Respond to comments/questions
- [ ] Share on personal networks
- [ ] Engage with early users
- [ ] Track metrics

### Evening
- [ ] Thank you message to community
- [ ] Day 1 summary post
- [ ] Plan Day 2 engagement

---

## Post-Launch (Week 1)

### Daily Tasks
- [ ] Respond to all comments
- [ ] Share user-generated content
- [ ] Engage on social media
- [ ] Monitor metrics
- [ ] Collect feedback

### Weekly Tasks
- [ ] Analyze launch performance
- [ ] Identify top channels
- [ ] Plan content based on feedback
- [ ] Follow up with leads
- [ ] Celebrate milestones

### Product Hunt
- [ ] Product name and tagline

### Social Media
- [ ] Twitter announcement thread

- [ ] Email template
- [ ] Press release (if applicable)
