## 💰 Paid Plans Summary

### Free Tier - $0/month
- 5 projects
- Basic features
- Community support
- Perfect for: Individual developers, students

### Pro Tier - $29/month
- Unlimited projects
- Priority support
- Private packages
- Perfect for: Small teams, freelancers

### Team Tier - $99/month per developer
- Team collaboration
- Compliance reporting
- Custom workflows
- Perfect for: Growing companies (10-50 devs)

### Enterprise Tier - $299-599/month per developer
- SSO integration
- On-premises option
- Dedicated support
- Perfect for: Large organizations (50+ devs)
