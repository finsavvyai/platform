## 🎯 Upgrade Flows

### Free → Pro
**Trigger**: User hits free tier limits OR after 7 days

**Flow**:
1. "Unlock More Features" prompt
2. Show Pro benefits
3. "Upgrade to Pro" CTA
4. Payment form
5. Immediate upgrade
6. Welcome email

**Key Message**: "Unlock unlimited projects and priority support"

### Pro → Team
**Trigger**: User needs collaboration OR compliance features

**Flow**:
1. "Need Team Features?" prompt
2. Show Team benefits
3. "Upgrade to Team" CTA
4. Payment form
5. Immediate upgrade
6. Team setup wizard

**Key Message**: "Collaborate with your team and meet compliance requirements"

### Team → Enterprise
**Trigger**: User needs enterprise features OR 50+ developers

**Flow**:
1. "Enterprise Features Available" prompt
2. Contact sales CTA
3. Sales call/meeting
4. Custom proposal
5. Contract signing
6. Enterprise setup

**Key Message**: "Get dedicated support and enterprise-grade features"
