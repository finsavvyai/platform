FinOps questions

Do I need engineering buy-in?
Yes — but minimally. Engineering swaps one environment variable (the provider API key). No code changes, no deploys beyond that.
Can we still use our existing OpenAI/Anthropic contracts?
Yes. ClawPipe sits in front of your existing provider accounts. Your enterprise rates, pre-paid credits, and BAAs remain in place.
How does billing data flow to our finance systems?
Export CSV or JSON on any schedule. SAML-based SSO + role-based access means only approved finance users see spend data. SOC 2 Type II readiness on request.
Does ClawPipe see our prompts or user data?
No. Prompt content is never logged. Only metadata (token counts, cost, model, team) is tracked. Cache uses SHA-256 hashes.
What happens when a team hits their budget cap?
You choose: hard stop (requests fail with a budget error), soft alert (requests continue but notify finance + team lead), or auto-downgrade (route to cheaper model until cap resets).
What's the free tier really?
1,000 requests/day across all features, forever. Enough to audit your current spend and prove ROI before any conversation about paid tiers.
