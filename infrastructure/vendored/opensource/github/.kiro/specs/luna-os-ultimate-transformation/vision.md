### **The Fragmented Tool Ecosystem**

Current solutions force developers into an impossible choice:

#### **Power vs. Accessibility**
- **LangChain/LlamaIndex**: Powerful but require coding expertise, no visual interface
- **Zapier/Make**: Visual but limited AI capabilities, generic experience
- **OpenAI/Anthropic**: Raw power but vendor lock-in, no workflow orchestration

#### **Development vs. Production**
- **Cursor/Windsurf**: Great for coding but no deployment infrastructure
- **Enterprise AI Platforms**: APIs without abstraction, complex cost management
- **Visual Builders**: Prototyping tools that can't handle production requirements

#### **Individual vs. Collaborative**
- **Most AI tools**: Single-user experiences with no real-time collaboration
- **No platform**: Combines AI development with team workflows like Figma does for design

### **The Context Crisis**

The deepest problem is that AI tools don't understand context:
- No memory of your project architecture or team conventions
- No understanding of your business logic or domain expertise
- No continuity between sessions or conversations
- No institutional knowledge capture or sharing
