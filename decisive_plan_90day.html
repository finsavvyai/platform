Scope frame

This is a 6-month execution plan, not a pure 90-day plan. The first 90 days prove the product: Brain skeleton, first agents, design-partner usage, Investigate migration, and SOC 2 kickoff. The second 90 days close the loop: paid conversion, ComplyAdvantage, SOC 2 Type 1, GA, and Series A.

The week-by-week schedule runs through W24, ending November 8. November 9-30 is the close buffer for legal, customer procurement, funding wires, and launch follow-through.
