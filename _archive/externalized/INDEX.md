Distinct from `_archive/portfolio-snapshots/` (which is "shut down / freeze"). Externalized = "kept alive, just not here".

| Repo | Disposition | Reason | Action plan |
|---|---|---|---|
| [looma-sh](looma-sh/) | SPIN-OUT | V2V automotive messaging. Live prod (`relay.looma.sh`), paid tiers, investor brief, IP-protection work underway. Off-thesis for AI-infra. | See `looma-sh/SPIN_OUT_PLAN.md` |

## Founder rationale

From the 2026-05-25 founder memo:

> The ecosystem thesis is **AI software infrastructure**. Looma is **automotive V2V messaging infrastructure**. Different vertical, different buyer, different regulatory landscape (DOT/NHTSA, not SOC 2/EU AI Act), different sales motion. Folding it into FinsavvyAI dilutes both stories. Killing it destroys real value. Spin-out preserves both.
