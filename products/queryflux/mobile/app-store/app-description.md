# App Store Descriptions

## App Name
Multi-Database Manager

## Subtitle (iOS) / Short Description (Android)
Monitor and manage your databases from anywhere

## App Store Description (iOS)

**Multi-Database Manager Mobile** is the essential companion app for database administrators and developers who need to monitor their database infrastructure on the go.

**🚀 Key Features:**

**Real-time Monitoring**
Keep track of all your database connections and their performance metrics in real-time. Monitor CPU usage, memory consumption, active connections, and query performance across all your databases.

**Instant Alerts**
Never miss a critical database issue again. Receive push notifications when problems arise, including connection failures, performance degradation, or resource exhaustion.

**Secure Access**
Connect safely to your database server with end-to-end encrypted communications. Your credentials never leave your device, ensuring maximum security for your sensitive data.

**Multi-Database Support**
Works seamlessly with:
• PostgreSQL
• MySQL/MariaDB
• MongoDB
• Redis
• SQLite

**Performance Insights**
Get detailed insights into your database performance with beautiful charts and metrics. Track trends over time and identify potential issues before they become problems.

**Quick Query Execution**
Execute simple queries and view results directly on your mobile device. Perfect for quick data checks and troubleshooting on the go.

**🎯 Perfect for:**
• Database Administrators managing multiple servers
• Developers who need quick access to database status
• DevOps teams monitoring production databases
• IT professionals who want peace of mind about their infrastructure

**📋 Requirements:**
This app requires the Multi-Database Manager desktop application to be running on your server. The desktop application provides the secure API that this mobile app connects to.

**🔒 Privacy & Security:**
Your privacy is our priority. Database credentials are never stored on external servers. All communication is encrypted and goes directly between your mobile device and your database server through your own infrastructure.

**✨ Why Choose Multi-Database Manager?**
• Native iOS design following Apple's Human Interface Guidelines
• Optimized for both iPhone and iPad
• Works offline with cached data
• Regular updates with new features
• Dedicated customer support

Download now and stay connected to your databases wherever you are!

## Privacy Policy Summary

**Data Collection:**
We collect minimal data necessary for app functionality including connection settings (stored locally) and anonymous usage statistics to improve the app.

**Data Usage:**
Your database credentials and connection information never leave your device. All communication is direct between your mobile device and your database server.

**Third-party Services:**
We use Firebase for push notifications and crash reporting. No personal data is shared with third parties.

**User Control:**
You can disable analytics and notifications at any time through the app settings.
