SOC 2 status

We're transparent about where we are. Last updated 2026-05-20.

Where we are right now

  • Type II audit: not yet engaged.
  • Trigger: we engage the auditor after the first ~$30K of yr-1 revenue clears. That funds the engagement; we don't want to take that money from runway.
  • Estimated start: Q3 2026.
  • Estimated report: Q1 2027 (Type II requires a 6-month observation window).

What we already have

We've implemented and operate the controls that map to the SOC 2 criteria; we just don't yet have an external auditor attesting to them.

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256 / customer-managed envelope).
  • RBAC + least privilege; admin actions audited.
  • HMAC-chained, tamper-evident audit log (see architecture).
  • Tenant isolation via Postgres RLS.
  • Vulnerability scanning + dependency scanning in CI; release blocked on Critical/High.
  • Backups: hourly WAL + nightly snapshot; tested quarterly restore.
  • Incident response runbook + disclosure inbox (security@sdlc.cc).

What's coming with the audit

  • Independent penetration test (annual).
  • Formal vendor-risk review for every sub-processor.
  • Quarterly internal control review.
  • Public Type II report — under NDA, available on signed MSA.

Bridge for procurement teams

If your procurement process needs a SOC 2 today and yours can't be deferred, talk to us. We've shipped two prior interim "controls letters" that procurement teams have accepted as a bridge to the eventual Type II.
