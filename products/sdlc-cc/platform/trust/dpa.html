DPA & MSA

One Master Services Agreement covers all three products. Last reviewed 2026-05-20. Lawyer-review pending — these are working drafts.

Structure

  1. Master Services Agreement (MSA) — the customer signs this once.
  2. Order Form per product — one of sdlc-platform · AMLIQ · Claw. Stack any combination.
  3. Data Processing Addendum (DPA) — bound to the MSA. GDPR Art. 28 + UK Addendum + EU SCCs (Module Two).
  4. Product-specific exhibits — DLP preset list, audit-log retention, SCIM scope, etc.

Standard terms

  • Data residency election: EU (default) or US.
  • Retention: prompts/responses default 30 days, configurable down to 0 (no retention) on Enterprise.
  • Sub-processors: see list; 30-day notice for additions.
  • Audit log: 13-month retention default; longer on request.
  • Incident notification: ≤72h from confirmation per GDPR Art. 33.

Negotiation policy

We accept reasonable customer redlines on the MSA and DPA. We do not redline core security controls (DLP, audit log, RLS) — these are product-level guarantees, not contract terms.

How to request

Email legal@sdlc.cc with company name, intended product(s), and any required jurisdiction. We respond within 2 business days with the working drafts.
