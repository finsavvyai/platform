### Usage Notes
- The icon should be recognizable at 16x16px (favicon) and 512x512px (app store)
- Prefer geometric over organic shapes to match the engineering/security positioning
- Avoid generic lock or shield clip art; integrate the security concept into the letterforms or abstract pattern
- Test the final logo against both dark (#0a0a0a) and light (#ffffff) backgrounds
