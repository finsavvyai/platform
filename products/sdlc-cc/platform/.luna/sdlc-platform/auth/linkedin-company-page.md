### About (2000 chars max)

SDLC.ai builds the Secure Data Learning Platform (SDLP) -- a zero-trust middleware layer between enterprise data and AI models.

We solve the hardest problem in enterprise AI adoption: how do you let teams use LLMs without exposing sensitive data, violating compliance, or losing control?

Our platform provides:

- Privacy-preserving RAG with automatic PII detection, redaction, and tokenization before data reaches any model
- A multi-provider LLM gateway with per-tenant rate limits, token budgets, prompt firewalls, and output sanitization
- OPA-based policy enforcement with autonomous policy learning that adapts to your organization's data patterns
- Full audit trails for every AI interaction -- who asked what, which documents were retrieved, what was redacted, and what was returned
- Row-level security across the entire stack, enforced at the database layer with tenant isolation by default

Built for fintech, healthcare, legal, and government organizations where data governance is not optional.

Technology: Go (gateway), Python + Rust (RAG engine), Next.js (admin console), PostgreSQL + pgvector (vector search), Cloudflare Workers (edge proxy), OpenAPI-driven SDKs in Go, Python, and TypeScript.

We are hiring engineers who care about security, performance, and shipping software that regulated enterprises can trust.
