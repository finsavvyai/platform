Frequently Asked Questions

How does SDLC.ai work without accessing my data?
SDLC.ai operates as a proxy between your application and AI providers. PII detection and redaction happens in-memory at the edge — your data is never stored on our servers. We use a zero-knowledge architecture: we process the request, redact sensitive data, forward the clean request, and immediately discard the original.
What happens if your service goes down?
We guarantee 99.99% uptime via our SLA. Our infrastructure runs across multiple availability zones with automatic failover. In the unlikely event of a complete outage, you can configure your integration to fail-open (pass requests directly to the AI provider) or fail-closed (block requests until compliance is restored).
Can I deploy SDLC.ai on-premise?
Yes. Enterprise plans include the option for on-premise or VPC deployment. We support AWS, Azure, and GCP private cloud environments. Our team handles the setup and provides dedicated support for your deployment.
Do you have a BAA for HIPAA compliance?
Yes. We provide Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) for all Professional and Enterprise customers. Our platform is designed from the ground up to handle PHI in compliance with HIPAA regulations, including automatic redaction of 12+ PHI data types.
How does SDLC.ai handle file attachments?
SDLC.ai scans all file attachments (CSV, JSON, TXT, PDF, and more) for PII before they reach any AI provider. The same detection engine that processes text also parses file contents, redacts sensitive data in-memory, and forwards only the clean version. This works automatically — no additional configuration needed.
What's the integration effort?
One line of code. Change your AI provider's baseURL to https://api.sdlc.cc/v1 — that's it. We're compatible with OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and all major AI SDKs. No code refactoring, no new dependencies, no migration project. Most teams are live in under 5 minutes.
