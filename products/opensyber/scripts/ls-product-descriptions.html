LemonSqueezy Product Setup

Click any field to copy. Create each product at app.lemonsqueezy.com/products/new

How to use

  1. Go to LemonSqueezy → Products → New Product
  2. Click each field below to copy, then paste into the LemonSqueezy form
  3. For each product: set name, description, subscription pricing, then publish
  4. After publishing, go to Variants tab to add the remaining 2 variants
  5. Repeat for the second product
