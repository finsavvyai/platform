# REGULATORY.md -- AML/CFT Regulatory Framework by Jurisdiction

## International Standards

| Framework | Body | Status | Key Requirements |
|-----------|------|--------|-----------------|
| FATF 40 Recommendations | FATF | Updated Nov 2023 | AML/CFT/CPF standards, risk-based approach |
| UN Security Council Resolutions | UNSC | Ongoing | Targeted financial sanctions (TFS) |

### FATF Grey & Black Lists (as of February 2026)

**Black List** (High-Risk, Call for Action): Iran, North Korea, Myanmar

**Grey List** (Under Increased Monitoring): Albania, Botswana, Bulgaria,
Cambodia, Cayman Islands, Croatia, DRC, Ghana, Haiti, Jamaica, Kenya,
Kuwait, Lebanon, Monaco, Namibia, Nepal, Papua New Guinea, South Sudan,
Syria, Venezuela, Vietnam, Virgin Islands (UK), Yemen

## United States

| Law/Regulation | Agency | Purpose |
|---------------|--------|---------|
| Bank Secrecy Act (BSA) 1970 | FinCEN | AML framework, CTR/SAR filing |
| USA PATRIOT Act 2001 | FinCEN | Enhanced CDD, Section 311/312 |
| OFAC Sanctions Programs | OFAC (Treasury) | SDN list, sectoral sanctions |
| Corporate Transparency Act 2024 | FinCEN | Beneficial ownership reporting |

**Key Lists**: OFAC SDN, OFAC Non-SDN/Consolidated, BIS Entity List

## European Union

| Law/Regulation | Status | Purpose |
|---------------|--------|---------|
| AMLD6 (6th Anti-Money Laundering Directive) | Adopted May 2024 | Harmonized AML rules across EU |
| EU AMLA (Anti-Money Laundering Authority) | Operational July 2025, Frankfurt | Central EU AML supervisor |
| EU Funds Transfer Regulation 2023 | In force | Crypto asset transfers (travel rule) |

**Key Lists**: EU Financial Sanctions (FSF), EEAS Consolidated

## United Kingdom

| Law/Regulation | Agency | Purpose |
|---------------|--------|---------|
| Proceeds of Crime Act (POCA) 2002 | NCA | Money laundering offences |
| Money Laundering Regulations (MLR) 2017 | FCA/HMRC | CDD requirements for regulated firms |
| Terrorism Act 2000 | Home Office | Terrorist financing offences |
| Sanctions and Anti-Money Laundering Act 2018 | FCDO/OFSI | UK sanctions framework post-Brexit |

**Regulatory Bodies**: OFSI (sanctions enforcement), FCDO (policy),
FCA (financial regulation), NCA (law enforcement)

**Key Lists**: UK Sanctions List (UKSL, replaced ConList Jan 28, 2026)
