# AMLIQ — "Pretty... Pretty... Compliant." Campaign

## Campaign Concept

Curb Your Enthusiasm-style: awkward truths, neurotic observations,
uncomfortable honesty about broken AML systems.

## Hero Video Script

Scene: Compliance officer at desk. 47 tabs open. Sweating.

"So I'm looking at this alert... and I don't know what it means...
but I also don't want to ignore it..."

Cut to clean AMLIQ dashboard.

"Oh. It just... tells me."
"That's it? That's the whole thing?"
