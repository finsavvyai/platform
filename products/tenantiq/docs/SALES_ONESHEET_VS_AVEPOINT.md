# TenantIQ vs. AvePoint License Optimization

> AI-powered Microsoft 365 license and cost optimization for MSPs

---

## Headline

**Stop bleeding Microsoft 365 spend while strengthening security and compliance.**

TenantIQ gives MSPs an AI-powered, multi-tenant view of license waste across all Microsoft 365 tenants, tightly integrated with security posture and compliance insights. Built for MSP owners, vCIOs, and operations teams who need to defend margins and prove value to clients.

---

## The Problem

- Microsoft 365 licensing is complex and constantly changing
- MSPs lose margin to unused, underutilized, and oversized SKUs
- Traditional tools focus on cost only, ignoring security and compliance risk
- AvePoint License Optimization is strong on license visibility and procurement, but lives largely in the licensing lane -- separate from day-to-day security, compliance, and automation workflows

---

## TenantIQ: Key Outcomes for MSPs

| Outcome | How |
|---------|-----|
| **Cut license waste across every tenant** | AI surfaces unused licenses, inactive users, and underutilized premium SKUs with concrete downgrade/removal recommendations and projected savings |
| **Tie cost optimization to risk reduction** | See license waste in context of CIS benchmarks, Secure Score, risky users, and compliance gaps -- prioritize actions that save money AND reduce risk |
| **Automate recurring optimization work** | License Autopilot runs on schedule, proposes changes, routes for approval, executes via workflows -- no more spreadsheets |
| **Tell a compelling ROI story** | Savings leaderboards, tenant-level ROI dashboards, and AI-generated executive summaries show clients exactly how you protect their budget |

---

## How TenantIQ Compares to AvePoint

| Dimension | TenantIQ | AvePoint License Optimization |
|-----------|----------|-------------------------------|
| Core focus | Unified security + compliance + cost intelligence, MSP-first | Dedicated license visibility module within AvePoint platform |
| License optimization | Per-user and per-SKU utilization, feature-level usage, AI downgrade/removal suggestions, savings leaderboard, cross-tenant views | Identifies unused/underused licenses, rightsizing guidance, centralized cost dashboards |
| Automation | License Autopilot with approval gates, event- and schedule-based optimization, integrated with onboarding/offboarding | Primarily dashboard-driven actions within AvePoint governance environment |
| Context | Optimization enriched with Secure Score, CIS benchmarks, risky users, compliance posture, anomaly detection | License-centric view; security/compliance handled by other AvePoint modules |
| Pricing motion | MSP-first per-skill pricing -- offer cost optimization, backup, CIS/compliance as separate packaged services | Traditional per-tenant/per-module SaaS aligned to AvePoint suite |
| Buyer value | One pane of glass for CISO, vCIO, and finance: cost savings + risk reduction + compliance automation | Strong for procurement/ops teams focused on spend across AvePoint-managed estates |
