# TenantIQ — 2-Minute Demo Script

Total runtime: ~2 minutes. Record at 1080p, dark mode enabled.

---

## Scene 1: Sign In (15 seconds)

1. Show the sign-in page with tagline: "M365 security, fully in control"
2. Click "Sign in with Microsoft"
3. Show the dashboard loading skeleton (brief)

**Voiceover**: "TenantIQ gives MSPs a single control plane for Microsoft 365
security, compliance, and cost optimization. Let me show you how."

---

## Scene 2: Dashboard Overview (20 seconds)

1. Highlight the four metric cards:
   - Secure Score: 66%
   - Total Users: 157
   - License Cost: $1,240/mo
   - Active Alerts: 4
2. Point out the quick actions row
3. Hover over the AI chatbot icon in the bottom-right corner

**Voiceover**: "Your dashboard shows tenant health at a glance — security
score, user count, license spend, and active alerts. Everything updates in
real time from Microsoft Graph."

---

## Scene 3: CIS Benchmark (20 seconds)

1. Navigate to Security > CIS Benchmark
2. Show the score ring: 41/100
3. Expand one control to reveal remediation steps
4. Scroll briefly to show the full control list

**Voiceover**: "Over 100 CIS controls are evaluated against your live
tenant. Each finding includes step-by-step remediation — or you can
auto-remediate with one click."

---

## Scene 4: Compliance Frameworks (15 seconds)

1. Navigate to Audit & Compliance
2. Show the SOC 2, HIPAA, and GDPR cards with scores
3. Click into one framework to show mapped controls

**Voiceover**: "Compliance frameworks map directly to your CIS results.
SOC 2, HIPAA, GDPR — audit-ready compliance in one click."

---

## Scene 5: License Optimization (15 seconds)

1. Navigate to Licenses
2. Show the waste analysis section
3. Highlight the savings amount

**Voiceover**: "Our license analyzer found $1,240 in monthly savings —
unused licenses, duplicate assignments, and downgrade opportunities."

---

## Scene 6: AI Agent (20 seconds)

1. Navigate to AI Agent
2. Show the suggested prompt cards
3. Type or select a prompt: "Which users have no MFA enabled?"
4. Show the AI response

**Voiceover**: "Ask anything about your tenants in plain English. The AI
agent queries your live data and returns actionable answers."

---

## Scene 7: One-Click Remediation (10 seconds)

1. Show an alert card with a "Remediate" button
2. Click to show the dry-run preview modal
3. Highlight the rollback option

**Voiceover**: "Fix security issues with a dry-run preview before
applying. Every action is reversible with built-in rollback."

---

## Scene 8: Call to Action (5 seconds)

1. Show the TenantIQ logo and tagline
2. Display URL: tenantiq.app

**Voiceover**: "TenantIQ — the Microsoft 365 control plane for MSPs.
Start your free trial at tenantiq.app."

---

## Recording Notes

- Use dark mode throughout for visual consistency
- Keep mouse movements deliberate and slow
- Pause briefly on each metric card for readability
- Record voiceover separately for clean audio
- Export at 1080p, 30fps, MP4 format
