## About (max 2000 chars)

TenantIQ is the AI-powered Microsoft 365 control plane for MSPs and IT teams managing multiple tenants at scale.

One dashboard secures, optimizes, and documents every tenant you manage — continuous CIS benchmark scanning across 100+ controls, SOC 2 / HIPAA / GDPR / Zero Trust compliance assessments, and per-tenant configuration drift detection with severity-classified alerts.

License optimization finds the money everyone else misses: per-SKU utilization, inactive-user detection, AI-generated downgrade recommendations (E5 → E3) with projected savings, and realized-savings leaderboards across your portfolio.

The built-in AI agent powered by Anthropic Claude answers natural-language questions about your tenants — "which users have risky sign-ins this week," "which licenses can we reclaim," "generate a SOC 2 evidence pack for tenant X" — with streaming responses and 13+ built-in tools.

Workflow automation handles recurring operations: user lifecycle (onboarding/offboarding across groups, mailboxes, MFA, licenses), guest reviews, nightly AES-256-GCM encrypted backups to Cloudflare R2, and cross-tenant migration wizards with pre-flight validation.

Built from day one for MSPs:
- Unlimited managed tenants per seat
- Cross-tenant benchmarking and savings leaderboards
- MSP-first pricing (per-tenant, not per-user)
- White-label and partner portal support
- PSA/RMM integrations (ConnectWise, Datto, Kaseya)

Deployed on Cloudflare's global edge — sub-100ms API response, zero-maintenance scaling, enterprise-grade security posture.
