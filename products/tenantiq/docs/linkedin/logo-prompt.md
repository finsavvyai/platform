## Brand guidelines for the generator

- **Palette**
  - Primary indigo: `#4F46E5`
  - Violet accent: `#7C3AED`
  - Electric cyan highlight: `#06B6D4`
  - Surface dark: `#0B0F17`
  - Surface light: `#F7F8FA`
- **Typography**
  - Display/logo: Inter Tight, Satoshi, or General Sans
  - Body: Inter
- **Do**: flat geometric, generous negative space, pixel-crisp, scalable, readable at 16×16.
- **Don't**: skeuomorphism, literal padlocks, generic cybersecurity clichés (hexagons, binary rain), drop shadows, bevels, photorealism, AI-generated faces.
