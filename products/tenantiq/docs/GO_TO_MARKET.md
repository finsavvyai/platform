- Offer a no-commitment CIS benchmark scan for one tenant
- Generate a branded PDF report showing security gaps

- Sponsor and present at MSP-focused events (IT Nation, DattoCon, ChannelPro)
- Partner with MSP peer groups (HTG, Taylor Business Group, ASCII)

- Interactive tool: "Enter your tenant count and average users"

## Summary

TenantIQ's go-to-market is built on three principles:

1. **Lead with value**: Free CIS scan gets prospects in the door. Immediate insights create urgency.
2. **Sell outcomes, not features**: MSPs care about margin, scale, and client retention -- not dashboards.
3. **Grow with the customer**: Start at $29/tenant, expand through add-on skills, graduate to enterprise + savings-based pricing.

The path to $1M ARR is 1,000 tenants at ~$100/tenant average. With the MSP market's natural multi-tenant structure, each customer brings 5-50 tenants -- meaning 20-200 customers to reach the target.
