# TenantIQ Growth Playbook

> From product to revenue: acquisition, activation, expansion

---

## Core Insight

MSPs don't buy tools. They buy margin increase, risk reduction, and time saved per tenant.

**Positioning**: "Increase your MSP margin per tenant in 30 days"

---

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

| Segment | Profile | Pain | Why They Buy |
|---------|---------|------|-------------|
| **Primary** | MSPs managing 10-200 tenants, using Lighthouse/CIPP/scripts | Manual work, inconsistent security, license waste | Switch-ready, immediate ROI |
| **Secondary** | Security-focused MSPs selling compliance (SOC2, ISO) | Audit prep, evidence collection, compliance gaps | Compliance automation, remediation |

---

## Growth Engine

```
Scan --> Insight --> Action --> Report --> Share --> New Lead
```

---

## Entry Wedge: Free Tenant Scan

**The #1 growth weapon.** "Scan your Microsoft 365 tenant in 60 seconds."

Output in <5 minutes:
- Security score with risk findings
- License waste (exact $ amount)
- Compliance gaps
- Top 5 fixes with one-click remediation

No commitment. Instant value. High conversion.

---

## Funnel

| Stage | Tactic | Target |
|-------|--------|--------|
| **Acquisition** | LinkedIn outbound, Reddit r/msp, YouTube demos, SEO | MSP founders, CTOs, vCIOs |
| **Hook** | "You are wasting $2,340/month on Microsoft 365 licenses" | Personalized = high conversion |
| **Activation** | Connect tenant, see risk + savings in <5 min | Must happen fast |
| **First Value** | Click "Fix all high-risk issues" -- instant ROI | One-click remediation |
| **Expansion** | Add tenants, enable automation, enable AI | Usage-based growth |

---

## Sales Motion

Product-led + assisted sales:
1. Self-serve onboarding (free scan)
2. "Book optimization call" after first value
3. Pitch: "We help MSPs reduce M365 risk and cut license costs -- and we prove ROI in the first scan"

## Viral Features (Growth Loops)

| Feature | Loop | Trigger |
|---------|------|---------|
| **Savings Report PDF** | MSP sends to client --> client asks "how?" --> referral | Built-in marketing |
| **Tenant Risk Leaderboard** | "You are the riskiest tenant" --> urgency --> upsell | Competitive pressure |
| **Before/After Snapshot** | "We improved security by 62%" --> social proof | Share-worthy |
| **AI Executive Report** | Weekly to CEO/CFO --> internal pressure --> expansion | C-suite visibility |
| **MSP Profit Dashboard** | Show cost/tenant, savings, margin --> "this makes me money" | Retention |
| **Benchmark Comparison** | "Bottom 20% of security" --> nobody wants to be last | Competitive fear |
| **One-Click Fix Share** | "Fixed 12 issues in 1 click" --> screenshot --> social | Viral proof |
| **Public Exposure Alert** | "37 publicly exposed files" --> panic --> action | Emotional trigger |
