## Recording Notes

- **Resolution:** Trailer, Explainer, Ad = 1920x1080. Social = 1080x1920 (crop the 390x844 phone frame or scale up).
- **Format:** Record as MP4 (H.264). Use 60fps for smooth animations.
- **Audio:** Layer voiceover on top in a video editor. Recommended: subtle ambient music underneath (e.g., Epidemic Sound or Artlist, look for "tech" or "corporate innovation" tracks).
- **Post-production:** Trim any extra time at start/end. Add audio fade in/out. Consider adding sound effects for card appearances and the checkmark animation.
- **Voiceover:** Recommended tone is confident, calm, and authoritative. Male or female voice. Moderate pace. Services: ElevenLabs, WellSaid Labs, or hire on Fiverr.
