## About (2000 chars max)

TenantIQ is an AI-powered security and compliance platform built for Managed Service Providers (MSPs) who manage Microsoft 365 tenants at scale.

Managing dozens -- or hundreds -- of M365 tenants means drowning in security alerts, compliance gaps, and license waste. TenantIQ replaces spreadsheets, manual audits, and disconnected tools with a single intelligent dashboard that continuously monitors, benchmarks, and optimizes every tenant you manage.

What TenantIQ does:

- CIS Benchmark Automation: 100+ controls evaluated continuously against CIS Microsoft 365 Foundations Benchmark. One-click remediation for failing controls.
- AI-Powered Threat Detection: Behavioral anomaly detection across sign-ins, mailbox activity, and permission changes. Get alerts before breaches happen.
- Compliance at Scale: Map tenant configurations to SOC 2, HIPAA, GDPR, and NIST frameworks. Generate audit-ready reports in seconds.
- License Intelligence: Detect unused licenses, optimize SKU allocation, and forecast costs across your entire portfolio.
- Copilot Readiness: Assess every tenant's readiness for Microsoft Copilot deployment with actionable recommendations.
- Multi-Tenant Command Center: Unified view across all managed tenants with per-tenant health scores, drift detection, and configuration snapshots.

Built for MSPs, not enterprises. TenantIQ uses per-skill pricing so you pay only for the capabilities you need. Start with security monitoring, add compliance automation later -- no per-user fees, no seat-based surprises.

Security-first architecture: Zero Trust by default. All data encrypted in transit and at rest. SOC 2 Type II compliant infrastructure. No customer data stored beyond what is needed for analysis.

TenantIQ is built by security engineers who spent years managing M365 tenants and got tired of stitching together five different tools to answer one simple question: "Are my tenants secure?"

The answer should be instant. Now it is.
