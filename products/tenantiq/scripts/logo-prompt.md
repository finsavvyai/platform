# TenantIQ -- Logo Generation Prompts

> AI image generation prompts for TenantIQ brand identity.

---

## Brand Parameters

- **Company:** TenantIQ
- **Industry:** Cybersecurity / MSP Tools
- **Colors:** Deep dark #060b0f (primary background), Emerald green #10b981 (accent)
- **Values:** Security, intelligence, control, multi-tenant architecture
- **Aesthetic:** Minimalist, modern tech, premium SaaS

---

## Prompt 1 -- Icon Mark (Primary)

Minimalist logo icon for a cybersecurity SaaS brand called TenantIQ. Abstract geometric mark combining a shield silhouette with a network node pattern, representing multi-tenant security intelligence. Deep black background #060b0f with emerald green #10b981 accent. Clean vector style, no gradients, no text. The icon should suggest protection, data flow, and interconnected systems. Flat design, suitable for favicon and app icon at 32px. Modern tech aesthetic inspired by Vercel, Linear, and Stripe brand design language. White background version and dark background version.

## Prompt 2 -- Icon Mark (Alternative: Letterform)

Minimalist logo icon using the letters "TQ" as a monogram for TenantIQ, a cybersecurity platform. The T and Q interlock geometrically to form a single cohesive mark. Emerald green #10b981 on deep dark #060b0f background. Clean sans-serif geometric letterforms with sharp edges. No decorative elements. The negative space between the letters subtly suggests a shield or lock shape. Vector flat design, works at 16px favicon size and 512px app icon. Inspired by the minimalism of Linear, Raycast, and Arc browser logos.

## Prompt 3 -- Wordmark

Clean typographic wordmark for "TenantIQ" -- a cybersecurity SaaS platform. Modern geometric sans-serif font similar to Inter or Plus Jakarta Sans. The "IQ" portion subtly differentiated in emerald green #10b981 while "Tenant" remains white #ffffff. Tracking slightly tight for a premium feel. No icons, no symbols, pure typography. Both dark background (#060b0f) and light background (#ffffff) versions. The design should convey intelligence, precision, and enterprise trust.

## Prompt 4 -- Icon + Wordmark (Horizontal Lockup)

Horizontal logo lockup for TenantIQ. Left side: minimal geometric icon mark -- an abstract shield composed of interconnected nodes in emerald green #10b981. Right side: "TenantIQ" in clean geometric sans-serif, white on dark or dark on white. Generous spacing between icon and text. The overall composition should feel balanced, modern, and premium. Suitable for website header, email signature, and presentation slides. Deep dark background #060b0f. Minimal, no gradients, no drop shadows, no 3D effects.

## Prompt 5 -- App Icon (Square)

Square app icon for TenantIQ, a cybersecurity dashboard app. Rounded corners (iOS/macOS style). Deep dark background #060b0f filling the square. Centered: a minimal emerald green #10b981 geometric mark -- an abstracted shield with a subtle circuit pattern inside. The mark uses only straight lines and right angles, no curves. High contrast, immediately recognizable at small sizes. Inspired by the icon design language of Things 3, Fantastical, and 1Password. No text in the icon.

## Usage Guidelines

- Icon mark must be legible at 16x16px (favicon)
- Maintain minimum clear space of 1x icon width around the mark
- Never place green icon on green background
- Never distort, rotate, or add effects to the logo
- Horizontal lockup minimum width: 120px
- On light backgrounds, swap white text to #060b0f dark
