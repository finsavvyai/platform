How OpenSyber Hardens Every AI Call

OpenSyber executes policy decisions before model execution so risky payloads never become incidents.

1. PII Detection

Automatically detect and redact sensitive data like SSN, emails, phone numbers, medical records

2. Compliance Check

Apply industry-specific policies (HIPAA, GDPR, FINRA) before sending to AI providers

3. Forward to AI

Send sanitized requests to your chosen AI provider with full functionality preserved

4. Audit Trail

Create cryptographic audit logs for compliance, reporting, and regulatory requirements
