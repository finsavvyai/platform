Do / Don't

Do

  • Lead with outcomes ("tests fixed themselves") before mechanics.
  • Use monospace for anything a developer would type.
  • Let the violet gradient appear once per view (logo or hero, not both).
  • Keep buttons rectangular with 10px radius — consistent with Apple HIG.
  • Pair Inter medium labels with mono values in data-heavy UI.

Don't

  • Stack violet + cyan + magenta in the same component.
  • Use brand-500 on white for body copy (fails AAA; use brand-800).
  • Use stock photos of people pointing at laptops.
  • Write headlines in monospace.
  • Add drop shadows or glows to the logo mark.
