# mabl (mabl.com)

> **Category**: AI-first / modern — enterprise AI-assisted testing with deep analytics
> **Threat level to Qestro**: **HIGH** — they have a real enterprise motion, 100+ enterprise customers, and the most advanced agentic-testing marketing positioning of any incumbent

## 1. One-sentence positioning
"AI Test Automation that Works for You" — agentic AI software testing that scales with growing teams. 8 years of AI-in-testing investment.

## 2. Core product capabilities
- **Agentic Tester**: digital teammate for test creation, execution, analysis, maintenance
- **AI-native testing lifecycle**: test generation, failure triage, healing
- **Test Semantic Search**: reuse existing test libraries via LLM search
- **Auto TFA** (Test Failure Analysis): autonomous failure triage
- **Auto-Healing**: reduce test maintenance
- Cross-browser testing (auto across multiple browsers)
- Data-driven testing
- Platforms: Web, Mobile (iOS + Android), API, AI apps
- Integrations: Jira, GitHub, GitLab, Jenkins, Postman, Slack, MS Teams, Segment, MCP Server
- SOC 2, Google Cloud Partner

## 3. What they do BETTER than Qestro
- **100+ enterprise customers**: Workday, JetBlue, Vivid Seats, Intuit, ADP — serious logos.
- **8 years of AI-in-testing R&D** — deepest domain experience in the space.
- **"AI apps" testing** as a listed category — they're targeting the same vibe-coding-AI-app wave as Qestro.
- **Test semantic search** is a real wedge for teams with 10,000+ tests.
- **MCP Server integration** shipped — they're moving into the agent ecosystem.
- **Enterprise trust signals**: SOC 2, GCP Partner, 100+ logos.

## 4. What Qestro does BETTER than them
- **Transparent pricing**. mabl is contact-sales only; Qestro tiers are public.
- **Developer-native workflow**. mabl is QA-director-sold; Qestro is engineer-adopted.
- **Self-serve**: Qestro signs up in 30 seconds. mabl needs a demo call.
- **Code ownership**: Qestro generates Playwright. mabl generates mabl-format tests.
- **Edge-native stack**: Cloudflare Workers + D1 = fast dashboards anywhere. mabl is centralized cloud.
- **Price point**: Qestro Pro is $499/mo publicly. mabl is almost certainly 10x+ that.

## 5. Tech stack signals
- Proprietary test DSL (JSON-based)
- Cloud-hosted (GCP, based on partnership)
- SOC 2 Type II
- No public GitHub; proprietary
- Heavy ML investment — they have an ML team producing academic-adjacent blog content

## 6. Pricing tiers
**Contact-sales only.** Public page says "simple, flexible, scalable" and "500 credits/month for cloud test runs" as the base. No public dollar amounts.
- Free trial available.
- Add-ons: advanced AI, content validation (email/PDF), Technical Account Manager.

## 7. Target customer
Mid-market to enterprise QA teams. Fortune 500 adjacent. Heavy regulated industries (finance, healthcare, retail, aviation). Buyer is VP QA or QA Director. Not a developer sale.

## 8. Recent signals
- MCP Server integration released — they're serious about agent ecosystem.
- "Agentic Tester" rebrand — competing for the same narrative real estate as Qestro and Autify.
- Blog cadence strong, thought leadership present.
- 100+ enterprise customers is a defensible moat.
- Funding: raised $77M total (public), last round $40M Series D 2021.

## 9. Qestro's winning angle vs mabl

**mabl is the 10-year-old enterprise incumbent of AI testing. They have the most convincing agentic narrative of any incumbent, and they're the single most credible threat in this analysis.** Direct comparison is risky — we will lose head-to-head RFPs.

**The move is to outflank, not outfight.** Mabl's weakness is their sales motion: they can't profitably sell to the solo developer or Series A startup. That's Qestro's native market.

"mabl is the enterprise choice for Fortune 500 QA directors. If that's you, go buy mabl. If you're a 5-person eng team shipping with Cursor and you need tests that write themselves, Qestro is built for you. We'll meet you in the enterprise when you're 500 engineers — until then, mabl's sales cycle will cost you more time than the tool will save you."

**Key wedge plays vs mabl**:
1. **Bottom-up adoption**: Qestro is installed by the engineer, not the QA director.
2. **Price point**: self-serve under the corporate card threshold.
3. **Vibe coding native**: mabl's AI is for test maintenance. Qestro's AI is for "I just described the test, now run it."
4. **Playwright code output**: mabl locks you in. Qestro's tests are git-committable Playwright code.

**Where Qestro loses to mabl**:
- 10,000+ test suites where semantic search matters.
- Regulated enterprise deals that require their 8-year track record.
- Global 2000 procurement.
- Avoid these deals for now; grow the SMB/mid-market base.

**Watch signal**: mabl launching a self-serve SMB tier. If they do, they become a direct competitor in Qestro's native market. High-priority monitor.
