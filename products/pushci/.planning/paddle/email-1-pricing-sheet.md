## Self-serve plans (published on /pricing)

| Plan       | Price           | Billing unit | Target                                  |
|------------|-----------------|--------------|-----------------------------------------|

| Pro        | $9              | per month    | Individual developers, multi-project    |

## Enterprise plan — custom pricing ranges

Enterprise is negotiated per-customer. Published guidelines:

| Component                        | Range                                     |
|----------------------------------|-------------------------------------------|
| Per-seat base                    | $50 – $150 / user / month                 |
| Dedicated single-tenant add-on   | +$2,000 – $8,000 / month (infra + ops)    |
| EU data residency                | +$1,000 / month                           |
| 24/7 incident response SLA       | +$2,000 / month                           |
| Minimum annual contract value    | $25,000                                   |
| Typical annual contract value    | $40,000 – $250,000                        |

Volume discounts apply above 100 seats. Multi-year contracts available
with prepayment discounts of 10–20%.

## What the Enterprise plan includes

- Everything in the Team plan
- Unlimited cloud minutes
- Dedicated single-tenant deployment option (EU or US region)
- 7-year audit log retention
- Bridges for Gerrit, Jenkins, AWS CodePipeline
- SCIM provisioning
- 24/7 incident response SLA
- One-time onboarding assistance (remote, scoped by contract)

## Notes

- All prices in USD. VAT / local tax handled by Paddle as merchant of record.
- Refund policy: 14-day money-back on self-serve plans; Enterprise per contract.
  Full terms at https://pushci.dev/refunds.
- Usage metering is transparent — customers can view real-time consumption
  in the dashboard.
