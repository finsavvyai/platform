## Core product: software-only SaaS

PushCI is a software product. The core offering is a CI/CD platform
delivered as:

- a CLI tool (distributed via npm, Homebrew, curl, GitHub Releases)
- a cloud-hosted API (Cloudflare Workers)
- a web dashboard (app.pushci.dev)
- a public marketing + docs site (pushci.dev)

Customers self-serve: install the CLI, connect their repository, and the
product runs autonomously. **No human labor is bundled into any self-serve
plan** (Free, Pro, or Team).

## Support — included in all paid plans

- **Free:** community forum + GitHub issues
- **Pro / Team:** async email support (response within 1 business day)
- **Enterprise:** 24/7 incident response SLA via shared Slack channel

Support is scoped to troubleshooting our software — we do not perform
development, configuration, or consulting work as part of standard support.

## Enterprise plan — scoped onboarding assistance

The Enterprise plan includes **one-time onboarding assistance** to help
the customer connect their existing systems (e.g. configure a Gerrit
bridge, map SSO providers, wire audit-log export to their SIEM).

- Remote only (video calls + documentation)
- Scoped by contract, typically 8–40 hours over the first 90 days
- Not billed as a recurring fee; included in Enterprise ACV
- Does not include custom feature development, managed operations, or
  ongoing consulting

The product remains the principal deliverable; onboarding is ancillary
and time-bounded.

## What we do NOT offer

- Managed CI/CD services where we operate the pipelines on the customer's behalf
- Custom software development billed as a service
- Consulting engagements separate from a product subscription
- Staff augmentation or contract engineers

## Summary

For Paddle's human-services classification: **PushCI is a software-only
SaaS product.** Enterprise onboarding is a bounded, complimentary
professional service included in the subscription fee, not a separate
service line.
