How to Use This Page

  1. Go to app.lemonsqueezy.com and open your store (ID 214097).

  3. Click any field below to copy its value, then paste it into the corresponding LemonSqueezy field.

  6. Set both variants to Subscription billing with Monthly interval.
  7. Enable "Thank You" page redirect to https://pushci.dev/welcome.
  8. Set the webhook endpoint to https://api.pushci.dev/webhooks/lemonsqueezy.
